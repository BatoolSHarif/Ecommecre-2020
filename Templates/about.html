SUSTAINABLE GROWTH

Investing for the future

The main financial principle of Lavender is to grow by using our own resources. In other words, we earn our money before we spend it. This makes it possible for us to make long-term investments for the future.

The Lavender vision is the foundation for our growth. We want to make sure that Lavender is accessible, so that more people can create a better everyday life at home. We re-invest a majority of our profits in existing and new Lavender stores, as well as in product development,

sustainable solutions and by continuously lowering prices to our customers.
